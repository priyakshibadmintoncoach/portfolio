About & Experience

Dedicated coaching for kids, beginners and confident first steps.

Priyakshee Borthakur builds training around patience, repetition and clear communication. Her coaching philosophy is simple: help young and new players enjoy the court while learning correct technique, movement patterns and match confidence.

Professional Bio

Calm instruction with academy-level discipline.

As Assistant Coach and Co-owner at P&B International Badminton Academy in Kolkata, Priyakshee works closely with players who are still developing their base. She emphasizes safe movement, repeatable technique and small milestones that keep students motivated.

Her experience spans local academy training, an international coaching stint in Dubai and direct mentorship under an international player, giving her a rounded perspective on player development.

Academy Management

Alongside on-court coaching, Priyakshee supports academy operations, oversees day-to-day training flow and helps manage logistics so players, parents and coaches have a dependable training environment.

  • Training batch coordination
  • Student progress oversight
  • Academy scheduling and logistics
  • Parent and business communication
